Howdy !
I am a PhD Candidate in Embedded Systems & Codesign Lab in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, College of Engineering at Texas A&M University, College Station. I am advised by Prof. Rabi Mahapatra. My research broadly focusses on integrating ideas from machine learning, parallel and distributed computing, and hardware accelerators for machine learning. Currently, I have been working on building fast, memory-optimized and communication-efficient algorithms to train large-scale distributed machine learning models at the "edge". I am also working towards designing hardware accelerators based on above algorithms with applications in embedded edge analytics.
I completed my Bachelor of Technology at Indian Institute of Technology(IIT), Guwahati majoring in Electronics and Communication Engineering with minor in Computer Science and Engineering.
